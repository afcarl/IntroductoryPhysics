# Description  
Miranda Cheng is a mathematician and theoretical physicist at the University
of Amsterdam. She works with the theoretical concept of Umbral Moonshine,
which works with the presence of shadows in the theory of mock modular forms.
Her background includes dropping out of high school in Taiwan to be part of a
punk rock band at the age of 16 and then later entering university as a part
of a gifted program. She now works as an assistant professor at the University
of Amsterdam and does work connecting to K3 surfaces and string theory. She is
able to pass on excitement and knowledge to students and expand the field of
theoretical physics as is related to mathematics.
