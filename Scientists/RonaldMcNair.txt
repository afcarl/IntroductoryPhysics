# Name

# Description  
McNair was an American physicist and an astronaut for NASA. He only lived
until the age of 36, for he died during the launch of the Space Shuttle
Challenger in 1986. He received his bachelors from North Carolina A & T State,
and received doctorates from MIT, NC A&T State, Morris College and Univ. or
South Carolina. McNair was a 5th degree black belt in karate and a performing
Jazz saxophonist.
